Joseph English - Musician

Springfield, Oregon.

Background

I am a musician and aspiring web designer with a lifelong love of internet culture. I make acoustic folk music under the moniker "Joe Fothergill" and I make weird electronic music for fun.

I studied music performance and composition in college, and while I love making music of all kinds, I have been making amateur websites since I was a teenager, and it makes sense to solidify those skills.

Goals

My aim is to become fluent in the following:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Ruby
  • Rails

I would like to make web design my career within the next few years. My hope is to work at a company creating and managing their online presence.
